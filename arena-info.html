About HI-LIFE Arena

The Hi-Life arena is an indoor amphitheatre located at Merrion road Dublin, Ireland.

Hi-Life Arena is a multipurpose venue inaugurated in 2024. Its creator, Marcelo Ferreira Neto, brought the most modern facilities in the world to this arena located on Merrion Road in Dublin, with a focus on concerts and performances. It comfortably accommodates up to 10,000 spectators. The arena features a 3D model where attendees can select their desired section to purchase tickets, and before finalizing the purchase, customers can access 3D photos of their seat and the view they will have of the main stage. The arena can host both private and public events for audiences starting from 20 spectators. It has 20 sectors, including lounges, with a main lounge (front stage) that can also be divided into 8 private lounges. All lounges have exclusive bars and quick access to restrooms.

Events you can host at the arena include:

*Concerts,

*Theater plays,

*Circuses,

*Sporting events,

*Corporate parties,

*Graduations,

*Weddings.
