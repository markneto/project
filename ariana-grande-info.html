Eternal Sunshine Tour

Ariana Grande, the globally renowned pop icon, is bringing her much-anticipated Eternal Sunshine Tour to Ireland. With her incredible vocal range and captivating stage presence, Ariana promises a show that's both visually and musically spectacular. The tour supports her latest album "Eternal Sunshine", showcasing her evolution as an artist and performer.

Tickets for the concert will be available from May 20, 2024. Make sure to secure your spot for an unforgettable evening with Ariana Grande.
