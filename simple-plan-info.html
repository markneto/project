Hard as Rock Tour

After a thrilling 15 years, Simple Plan is making a comeback with their "Hard as Rock" tour. Known for their energetic performances and catchy melodies, the band plans to bring an unforgettable night of music to Ireland. Get ready to experience the magic of live music once again with Simple Plan.

Tickets will be on sale soon. Stay tuned for an announcement on our website and make sure you don't miss out on the opportunity to see Simple Plan live.
